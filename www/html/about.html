YTParty

Watch YouTube with friends!

How YTParty works!

When you join a room, you will see the video currently playing, as well as a chat window at the side.

You may use the chat to talk about the video, and you may use some commands to add effects to your message! (use /help to see the commands)

You can open the Player Menu using ctrl+m where you can copy the room URL, change the current video and queue videos to play automatically one after another!

When someone in a room starts a video, the video will automatically play, pause and skip to any time across all the connected users!

How to create a room!

To create a room, you will need a YouTube video URL.

1) Navigate to YouTube and find a video you want to watch

2) Log into your account, or create one, and click the 'Create Room' button from the home menu

3) Enter the URL of the video you picked out. You may also want to change the room's colour theme and the player controls for other members.

4) Open the player menu with ctrl+m and press the 'Copy Room URL' button. Optionally, you can copy the URL from the URL bar.

5) Share the URL you just copied to allow other people to join your room!

How to join a room!

To join a room, you will need a room URL. Once you have the room URL, simply follow the link and you will automatically be signed in if you are not already!

1) Click the room URL that you will have been shared by another user

2) If prompted, sign into your account, or create an account and then click the link again

3) You should automatically be redirected to the room
