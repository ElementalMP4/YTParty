YTParty

Watch YouTube with friends!

What is YTParty?

YTParty is a social platform which allows you to watch YouTube videos as a group! Simply create a free account, choose your video and create a room!

How does it work?

When you choose your video, you must first create a watch room. Once you have made a room, share the unique room URL with your viewing party. Then, hit play and everyone's video player will start at the same time! When you pause, play and skip along the timeline, everyone will remain synchronised.

What else can it do?

The chat features a huge variety of fun commands you can try, which allow you to add effects to your messages, queue videos and more!
